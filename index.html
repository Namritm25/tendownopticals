About Us

Ten Down Opticals is a one-stop shop for all your vision and hearing needs, offering a wide range of high-quality products and services designed to enhance your eye and ear health. Our store provides expert eye exams, personalized eyewear fittings, and a variety of prescription glasses, contact lenses, and stylish sunglasses. We also offer specialized services like kids' eye care, low vision aids, and lens coatings such as anti-reflective, blue light protection, and UV protection.

  • Spectacles & Prescription Glasses
  • Contact Lenses & Sunglasses
  • Lens Coatings & Add-Ons (AR, Blue Light, UV)
  • Specialty Eyewear & Kids' Eye Care
  • Eye Health Consultations
  • Hearing Aids & Accessories
  • Hearing Aid Adjustments & Fittings
  • Hearing Assessments and Audiology Tests
